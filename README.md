Bank Loan Approval Analysis Dashboard
* Overview
-This project presents a comprehensive analysis of bank loan approval data to evaluate lending performance, credit risk, and customer segmentation trends. The dataset spans 2016â€“2026 and captures the effects of economic fluctuations, including the COVID-19 period and subsequent recovery. The objective is to support data-driven decision-making in banking and financial services.

* Business Objective

* Financial institutions require consistent monitoring of loan approval behavior to:
-Assess credit risk exposure
- Understand customer income and loan type dynamics
- Identify performance shifts during economic disruptions
- Improve lending strategy and portfolio quality

* Analytical Approach
- Analyzed 250K+ loan application records across multiple years
- Segmented data by income category, loan type, risk score, and year
- Designed a dynamic Excel dashboard using:
- Pivot Tables
- KPI metrics
- Slicers for time-based analysis
- Interactive charts

* Compared pre-COVID, COVID-impact, and post-COVID recovery trends

* Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Total Loan Applications
- Approved Loan Count
- Approval Rate (%)
- Average Credit Score
- Average Risk Score of Approved Loans
  
* Key Findings
- Loan approval rates declined during COVID-affected years, reflecting tighter credit policies
- Higher-income customers consistently demonstrated stronger approval outcomes
- Risk score remains a primary determinant of approval decisions
- Post-pandemic years show gradual recovery in application volume and approval rates
- Loan type performance varies annually, aligning with market demand and economic conditions
